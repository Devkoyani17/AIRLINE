Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and their answers:

  • How can I change my booking?

    You can change your booking by logging into your account or contacting our support team directly.

  • What should I do if I lost my ticket?

    If you’ve lost your ticket, contact support immediately. We’ll help you re-issue it quickly.

  • How do I cancel my flight?

    You can cancel your flight online via your dashboard or by speaking with a customer representative.
